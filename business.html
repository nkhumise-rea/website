DISRUPTIVE CLIENT-CENTRIC BUSINESS MODEL


Qualifying Organizations

  1. Aim to be at the forefront of their industry using technology.
  2. Seek to modify their business model to adapt to technological advancements.
  3. Require a fresh pair of eyes & have barriers adopting necessary technologies.

My Contributions

  1. Assist with developing an adaptive business strategy with focus on customers.
  2. Do the heavy lifting and verification of the model before your company invests in it.
  3. Manage the execution of the model, and oversee process efficiencies during execution.

My Responsibilities

Research

Conduct an independant study regarding your industry, consumers, and technologies that may be resourceful.

Desirability Study

Identify Market Loopholes. Re-establish the needs and interests of your consumers, particularly identify the rate of change of their desires in your industry.

Feasibility Study

Identify Organizational Strengths. Assess your strengths and position with respect to consumers' interests, and how best to meet them.

Business Model Design

Design the business strategy using the agile methodology.

Viability Study

Rapid prototyping & verification. Break the model into easily verifiable components, and engage with consumers to measure their desire towards it.

Cost Analysis

Perform cost estimation and planning along with timelines of implementing the model.

Contractual Advice

Provide continual support and guidance on resources and service providers to collaborate with, for you to reach your goal.

Execution Management

Manage contracts and oversee that the goal is achieved to your satisfaction.

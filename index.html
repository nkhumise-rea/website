About me

I find thrill in “understanding the unknown,” what you may call discoveries and subsequently creating “resources through my imagination,” also known as inventing. I began my journey with studying mechanical engineering, at Wits University , to learn the design of physical objects. Delved into control systems and robotics during my masters program at Tennessee Tech University , with the aim of learning system automation. Currently, I’m pursuing a PhD in AI, at Sheffield University , to train as a [computer] scientist and effectively master embodied intelligence.

My interests are insanely wide and cross-disciplinary ranging from maths, machine learning, computational psychiatry, fluid mechanics, mechatronics, robotics, medical robots, software to business. These constitute fields that offer scope for innovation, unresolved challenges, and meaningful human impact, while also sparking my curiosity. My hobbies include drawing/painting, creative writing, performing comedy or at least trying to - with playful pranking, and overexplaining things to my family and friends. I enjoy travelling and adventure, but don’t expect to see pictures on social media - because I'm bad at taking pictures and documenting my life 🙂!

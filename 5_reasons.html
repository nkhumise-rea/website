5 REASONS YOU NEED A DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION SPECIALIST


  • by: Rea Nkhumise
  • date: March 31, 2020

Digital transformation can be defined as the change in the company's model to deliver value to its customers using technology. This change needs to be coordinated by a digital transformation specialist for the following reasons:

1. Save You Stress

It's a daunting task to keep up with the latest technologies & their application in your business. A DX specialist can make the process easier and stress-free by breaking down their benefits specifically for your industry.

2. Save Money

A DX specialist can help you have accurate projections of the costs involved in adopting the new business model and/or technologies in your organization.

3. Look at the bigger picture

A DX specialist can match your current and future business needs with emerging technology. Oversee and manage every step of the project to ensure that your needs are met.

4. Deliver alternative ideas you might not have thought of

An experienced DX specialist will have the necessary years of experience in developing innovative technologies & consumer-centric business models. He/she will have a broader perspective and understanding of what is relevant for your company.

5. Great negotiator

A DX specialist can negotiate contracts and communicate terms better with stakeholders, developers, engineers, UI/UX designers, data scientists, and technical contractors because of his/her experience in the field.

Overview of responsibilies of a digital transformation (DX) specialist

Since you know what value a DX specialist brings to your company, let's establish his/her responsibilities.

- Establish client’s requirements & undertake feasibility study
- Undertake cost analysis
- Process tender & contract documents, including bills of quantities
- Perform risk, value management & cost control
- Advise on procurement strategy
- Identify, analyze & develop responses to commercial risks
- Allocate work to subcontractors
- Advise on contractual claims
- Analyze outcomes and write outcomes in detail
- Maintain awareness of various technologies valuable to the client’s needs
- Verify the developed systems or models
- Validate that the client’s needs are met
